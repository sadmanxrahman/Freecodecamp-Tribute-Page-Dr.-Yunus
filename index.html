Dr. Muhammad Yunus

The Poor Mans Economist


Timeline of Dr. Yunus


  • Born 28 June 1940 in Chittagong, Bangladesh.

  • Ranked 16 out of 39,000 in his matriculation exam.

  • He completed his BA in 1960 and MA in 1961. Both from the Department of Economics at Dhaka University.

  • In 1976, Yunus lent US$27 of his money to 42 women in the village, who made a profit of BDT 0.50 (US$0.02) each on the loan. Thus, Yunus is credited with the idea of microcredit.

  • He was awarded the Nobel Peace Prize in 2006 for his efforts to create economic and social development.
